About Me

I'm a Bengaluru-based Computer Science Engineering student at New Horizon College Of Engineering with a passion for photography that spans over several years. I specialize in creating images that are both visually striking and emotionally impactful, and I have developed a signature style that blends technical precision with creative vision.

When I'm not coding or behind the camera, you can find me exploring Bengaluru's vibrant tech scene, discovering new cafes, or spending time with friends. I believe that every moment is an opportunity to capture something extraordinary and to create memories that will last a lifetime.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me and my work. If you're interested in collaborating or would like to see more of my portfolio, please don't hesitate to get in touch. Let's work together to bring your vision to life!
